User Guidance

make your way better

Hi Welcome to InquiaMaK Inquiry Making system. You are facilitated to report your problems regarding public transportation services in Sri Lanka via Mr.bot. Your inquiry is supposed to include any problem regarding public transportation services (Bus and Train) in Sri Lanka. Your problem will be succefully reported to responsible authorizations within a day.

You can log into your account using your username and password. All usernames and passwords are secured within the system. Interactive chat bot requires to type 'hi bot' to start the chat. Then bot asks your inquiry and you are supposed to type valid inquiries. By providing correct answers for bot's quistions you can complete your inquiry. Bus numbers should be formatted as XXX-YYYY(XX-YYYY) (X is an alphebetic letter and Y is an integer).Any registered bus number with a route permit is acceptable. All required dates should be formatted as DD/MM/YYYY (eg:03/04/2018, 23/12/2017). You are allowed to make inquiries within a year. All time inputs should be formatted as XX:XX (eg:3:44,5:23,2:09,14:43). Any invalid input is seggested a cancelation. After canceling proceedure you can start a new conversation. Before saving your valid inquiry bot will show the complete inquiry and you are supposed to confirm or cancel it.
